Human Resource Services

Technology has changed the applicant's journey from checking job posts up to employment offer! Assessment, interviews and even training can all be done online.

When the recruitment data is all over the place and real time reporting is an issue, our smart recruitment solution can help! Our Talent Acquisition and Retention System is a one stop shop application that accommodates job posting, application, assessment, employee referral and real time updates!

Robotics Process Automation (RPA)

Payroll - The Payroll process can be tedious with lots of manual work and several tabs and files to consolidate. Also the payslips have to be received timely and routed to the right recipient. We have designed a Smart Payroll process where generation of individual payslip, conversion to a zipped file, and password assignment are all done by Chihiro, an RPA Bot. This automation has a 97% AHT reduction.

Order to Cash - When customer payments are not updated against their invoices and impacts the services they receive, this leads to complains and unnecessary escalation that may even lead to loss of business. Improving payments matching will help keep customers loyal as they do no need to make unnecessary contacts to validate payments. As for chasing payments, data analytics can help categorize customer types and payment patterns. Data driven strategies will be done from team assignment of skilled collectors and deployment of Bots, to support fast payment matching and improve customer experience!

Travel Expense Reimbursement - Business travel can be fun but definitely, not the reimbursement process!

Keeping track of expense reports and paper works are tedious and burdensome as it takes time to sort, validate and process reimbursements after travel receipts are submitted. This causes frustration for some of the employees waiting for reimbursement and for the department trying to keep track of expense reports!

Our RPA solution can improve turn-around time in the validation, and expense posting processes which will eliminate the back and forth, and long waiting time. Our team of experts ensure that policies are being adhered to and finds ways to introduce continuous improvement and controls.

Order Management - Adding to cart should be smooth and hassle- free experience for customers and vendors alike!

Outsourcing your order management process helps ease the task of managing the database of orders and never missing out on an opportunity to generate income due to missed-out orders and frustrated customers.

Our team can work better by introducing RPA Bots that will automatically send customer notifications, input data in ERP, as well as download reports! Clients can also benefit from our B2B and B2C best practices from other accounts!

Data Entry - Traditional ways of doing a certain task is already obsolete! Our smart data entry service scheme utilizes technology driven applications aiming for operational efficiency.

Data entry can be interesting and efficient if done with a Bot! The Bot does the heavy work and the Data Entry analyst will just check for accuracy. Another layer of check is done by the QA while and SME handles all customer interactions.
